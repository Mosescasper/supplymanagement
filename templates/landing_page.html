Core Modules

Everything Supply Chain needs to stay the source of truth.

Six modules cover the full loop: from stock on the shelf to the requisition that moved it.

Inventory Management

SKU-level items with unit cost and category. Live stock-on-hand, configurable reorder levels, and a full audit trail on every adjustment.

Supplier Management

A directory of suppliers with contacts and preferred-item links, plus a full purchase order history per supplier at a glance.

Purchase Orders

Multi-line POs with auto-generated numbers. Draft → Sent → Received → Cancelled — and receiving one restocks and logs the movement automatically.

Requisitions & Approvals

Any department raises a request; Supply Chain approves, rejects or holds it. Fulfilment deducts stock and logs the movement in one step.

Dashboard

Real-time KPIs — items, low-stock count, open POs, pending requisitions, stock value — with a live movement feed and status filters.

Reports & Analytics

Export inventory valuation, movement history and PO logs to CSV or Excel — filterable by category, supplier, date range and stock status.

Requisition & Approval Flow

From department request to stock issued.

A real sequence, end to end — every step logged against the item it touches.

01

Submit

Department staff raise a requisition with items and quantities needed.

02

Review

Supply Chain checks the request against current stock levels.

03

Decide

Approve, reject, or hold — the requester is notified either way.

04

Fulfil

Approved items are issued; stock is deducted and the movement logged.

05

Reorder

If stock runs low, the item surfaces on the dashboard for a new PO.
